About Chandra Villa

Nestled in the serene surroundings of Nathdwara, Rajasthan, Chandravilla is a luxurious 4BHK villa that promises an unforgettable escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Just a short drive from Udaipur, the City of Lakes, this exclusive retreat blends village vibes with modern comforts, offering guests a chance to unwind amidst nature’s beauty and stunning sunsets. Chandravilla is designed for relaxation and fun, featuring a private swimming pool and a variety of games to keep everyone entertained—think pool table, badminton court, cricket space, carrom board, and chess. The villa boasts spacious luxury rooms with elegant interiors, perfect for soaking in the calm ambiance and creating cherished memories. Surrounded by lush greenery, it’s an ideal spot for families, friends, or couples looking to enjoy a laid-back yet upscale getaway. With its warm hospitality, recreational offerings, and picturesque setting, Chandravilla invites you to experience the charm of rural Rajasthan while indulging in the comforts of a premium villa retreat.

Nearby Attractions

Shrinathji Temple

Just 6km away in Nathdwara, this temple is famous for its 7-year-old idol of Lord Krishna, with a fascinating history dating back to the 14th century.

Statue of Belief

Just 8km away from Chandravillakotharia, this statue of Lord Shiva is the tallest in the world at 369 feet. It was inaugurated on October 29, 2022, and is located on a hillock called Ganesh Tekri.

Shreenathji Goushala

Just 6.5km away from Chandravillakotharia, this Goushala is a serene place where you can connect with nature and witness the care provided to the cows. It is situated 3kms from the Shrinath Ji Temple.

Shree Dwarkadhish Temple

Shree Dwarkadhish temple situated in Kankroli 12km away from Chandravillakotharia is a temple of Lord Shree Krishna, known for their famous traditional culture and "Raal".

Rajsamand Lake

Rajsamand Lake, a major city attraction 2km far from Dwarkadhish temple, features the famous "Noochoki Paal", a favorite tourist and couple spot with a large waterview and beautiful sunset view.

Eklingji Temple

23km far from our place, Eklingji temple is a center of attraction in Udaipur situated in Delwada, a temple of Lord Shiva worshipped by Shree Maharana Pratap, known as Ekling Deewan.

Haldighati

17.5km away from our place, this place holds the history of the Battle of Haldighati, with a museum and famous rosewater.

Kumbhalgarh Fort

35km away from Chandravillakotharia, Kumbhalgarh Fort is a piece of art by Maharana Kumbha, featuring incredible architecture and the Parshuram Mahadev temple.
